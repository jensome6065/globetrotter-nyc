Local Etiquette and Ordering Tips

  • Cash is king - Many legendary spots are cash-only. Carry $20-40 in small bills.
  • Counter culture - Counter seating is often faster and gives you more interaction with staff.
  • Tipping - Standard is 18-20% for table service. For takeout, 10% is appreciated.
  • Peak times - Weekend brunch gets crowded. Weekday mornings and late afternoons are calmer.
  • No reservations - Add your name and explore nearby while you wait.
